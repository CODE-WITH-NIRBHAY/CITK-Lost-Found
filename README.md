CITK Campus Lost & Found is a web-based platform designed to help students and staff at
Central Institute of Technology, Kokrajhar (CITK) easily report, track, and recover lost items.

Instead of relying on scattered WhatsApp messages or physical notice boards, this system provides one trusted digital space where lost and found items are reported with images, automatically matched, and verified by campus authorities.

The goal is simple: reduce recovery time and increase return rates.
